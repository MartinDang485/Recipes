Grilled Chicken Breast Recipe

Cooked Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Seasonings of your choice
  • Prongs
  • 10' carbon steel pan
  • Olive oil

Directions

  • Pat chicken dry and wash
  • Heat up pan
  • Season your chicken
  • Place olive oil in pan and cook chicken for 8-12 minutes
  • Cook until internal temp is 155 fahrenheit
  • Take chicken off and let rest for 5 minutes
