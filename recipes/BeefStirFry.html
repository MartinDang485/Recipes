Beef Stir Fry Recipe

Beef Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Bell Peppers
  • Broccoli
  • Rice
  • Utensil
  • Olive oil
  • Pan

Directions

  • Heat up pan and prep ingredients
  • Add oil to pan and put in veggies
  • Stir till golden brown
  • Take veggies out and put in beef
  • Cook beef fully
  • Put all cooked ingredients on top of a bowl of rice
